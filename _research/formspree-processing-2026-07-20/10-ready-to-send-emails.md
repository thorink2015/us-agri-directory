directly in the body. Copy the subject + body of each block into your
mail client from `eugen@agdronedirectory.com` and send. No placeholders
to fill.

Order is roughly: NEW listings first (7 blocks), then UPDATES on
existing listings (3 blocks). Update blocks use "your listing has been
updated" wording. Where an operator's owner-name is unknown, "Hi
there," is used.
